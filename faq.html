Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pest control services.

General Questions

What types of pests do you handle?

We provide pest control services for cockroaches, ants, bedbugs, termites, rats, mosquitoes, house flies, snakes, honeybees, and more.

Are your pest control treatments safe?

Yes, we use eco-friendly and safe pest control solutions that are effective yet non-toxic for humans and pets.

How long does a treatment last?

The effectiveness of our treatments varies. For example, cockroach gel lasts up to 6 months, while termite treatment comes with a 5-year warranty.

Do you offer same-day services?

Yes, same-day services are available based on slot availability. Please contact us for urgent requests.

Yes, we recommend cleaning affected areas and removing food items. For bedbug treatments, washing and sealing infested items is advised.

Post-Treatment Care & Follow-ups

What should I do after the treatment?

Avoid cleaning treated areas for a few hours to allow the pest control solution to work effectively.

Will I need a follow-up treatment?

Some pest infestations may require multiple treatments. We will inform you if a follow-up is necessary.

How can I prevent pests from returning?

Regular cleaning, sealing entry points, and periodic pest control treatments help prevent reinfestation.
